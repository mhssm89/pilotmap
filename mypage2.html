Use On/Off Timer
 Timer is available to turn off the map at night if desired. Enter 'Time Off' and 'Time On' values in settings below. Verify RPI's time is set correctly for your timezone. RPI time is listed in the header.
     Time Off : Enter the desired time to turn map off in 24 hour format. i.e. '9 pm' would be entered '21:00'.
     Time On : Enter the desired time to turn map on in 24 hour format. i.e. '8:30 am' would be entered '8:30'.
     Sleep Interrupt Time Length of time in minutes to temporarily turn on map during sleep mode when button is pushed. (Allowable range = 0 to 120 minutes).
     Display 'Sleeping' Message
 Display 'Sleeping' message on LCD/OLED display when map is in sleep mode.
